üêß About Penguin Meds

Penguin Meds is a progressive web app designed to help you track your marijuana and nicotine medicines in a simple, private, and secure way.

This app can run in your desktop or phone. It is meant to be installed (see below for instructions) on your phone. So if you use it on your desktop that is fine. If you run it on your phone that is fine. Just note that they do not share data. The data is kept within the browser's local storage. There is no internet required to run the app once it is installed on your phone. Once installed you could turn off cellular and wifi and it will still work properly.

üîí Your Privacy Matters
All data is stored locally on your device only. No internet connection required. No accounts, no login, no tracking.
